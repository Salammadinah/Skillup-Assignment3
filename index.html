Rice and Beans Recipe

Ingredients
  • Rice
  • Beans
  • Onions
  • Beans
  • Water
Method Of Preparation
  1. Pick the beans and rinse
  2. Add water to the pot and put on fire
  3. Dice onions nad add to the boiling water
  4. Add the beans to the water and cook till tender but not mushy, about 40-45 minutes under medium-high heat
  5. Rinse the rice and add to the beans
  6. Add salt to taste
  7. Cook until done
  8. Serve with your favourite sause, soup or stew.
